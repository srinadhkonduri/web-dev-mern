Steps to Join the Fitness Club

Joining Elite Fitness Club is simple. Follow the steps below to start your fitness journey.

  1. Complete the online membership registration form.
  2. Select a suitable membership plan.
  3. Make the payment using our secure payment system.
  4. Book your initial fitness assessment.
  5. Start attending gym sessions and group classes.

Fitness Club FAQ

Do you provide personal trainers?
Yes. Certified trainers are available. Visit our personal training page to book a session.
Can beginners join the gym?
Absolutely. Beginners can start with our Beginner Fitness Program.
